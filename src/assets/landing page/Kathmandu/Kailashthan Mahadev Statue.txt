Sanga (Kailashnath Mahadev Statue)
Location: Sanga (about 20 km east of Kathmandu, on the border of Bhaktapur & Kavrepalanchok)
Description: Home to the world’s tallest statue of Lord Shiva — standing 143 feet tall on a hilltop with stunning views of the surrounding hills and valley.
Things to Do:

Visit the Kailashnath Mahadev Statue

Visit the suspension bridge

Have Fun at Kathmandu Fun Park
